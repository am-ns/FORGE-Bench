- **`SB01`** `fan_startup_5rpm`

- **`SB02`** `fan_startup_15rpm`

  > A wind turbine accelerates from rest to 30 RPM over 5 seconds. Camera is static at base level looking up the tower. The blades should be substantially motion-blurred, approaching a disc-like appearance.
